Pasta Recipe

Description

A versatile Italian dish made with noodles, served with sauces like marinara or Alfredo.

Ingredients:

Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.), olive oil, garlic, tomato sauce or cream, salt, pepper, Parmesan cheese.

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente; drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan; saut√© garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce or cream; simmer.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and Parmesan before serving.
