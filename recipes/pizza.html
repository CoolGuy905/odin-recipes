Pizza Recipe

Description

A baked flatbread topped with cheese, sauce, and various toppings.

Ingredients:

Pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.), olive oil.

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella and desired toppings.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 15-20 minutes.
