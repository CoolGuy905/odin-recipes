Lasagna Recipe

Description

Layered pasta sheets with meat, cheese, and sauce, baked to perfection.

Ingredients:

Lasagna sheets, ground meat, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan, herbs.

Steps to prepare:

  1. Cook lasagna sheets as per package instructions; drain.
  2. Cook ground meat; mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer sauce, lasagna sheets, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 30-40 minutes.
